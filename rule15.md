2. **Definitions for Rule 15**  
   - **Distribution Lines**: Overhead or underground lines operating at distribution voltages to supply **two or more services**.  
   - **Distribution Trench Footage**: Total trench length used for underground cabling, excluding service trenches.  
   - **Excavation**: Includes trenching, backfilling, surface repairs, and landscape restoration.  
   - **Scenic Areas**: Defined as highways, parks, or areas of "unusual scenic interest" where overhead lines may require special considerations (e.g., visibility restrictions).  
   - **Seasonal/Intermittent Loads**: Services for temporary or seasonal use (e.g., resorts, cottages).  
   - **Mixed-Fuel New Construction**: Buildings using gas/propane **and** electricity, requiring special load definitions.

3. **Cost Recovery Mechanisms**  
   - **Net Revenue Multiplier**: A factor applied to non-residential loads to determine allowances.  
   - **Monthly Ownership Charge**: A percentage rate to recover costs of customer-financed facilities not fully utilized.  
   - **Net Revenue**: Revenues allocated to cover DLX costs, excluding energy, transmission, and other unrelated charges.

4. **Service Types**  
   - **Residential Development**: 5+ dwelling units in 2+ buildings on a single parcel.
